About

PHELPERS strives to provide a positive environment where people are able to freely (and if desired, anonymously) share problems and life deepest questions. While others, with similar experiences can help, encourage and give suggestions on how to overcome hard situations.

Download the app now to start. Available on Apple Store and Google Play. Just type "PHELPERS" and start today by finding peers' help.
